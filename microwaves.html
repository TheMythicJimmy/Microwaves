Microwaves

GE Microwave
The GE microwave is fairly cheap and has a classic simple look to it so you can't really go wrong with it.
Samsung Microwave
The Samsung microwave comes in at a pretty hefty price but offers a sleek modern look that will only fit in certain households.
LG Microwave
This microwave, like the last, is pretty expensive and offers a wide array of features such as compatibility with smart assistants. It also has a modern look to it but it is cheaper than the Samsung.
Breville Microwave
This microwave has a more rounded look to appeal to those who wish to return to simpler times when microwaves were just meant to heat up food. 3rd cheapest microwave on this list.
Insignia Microwave
This is the cheapest microwave on the list but it probably gets the job done. This is probably a microwave that they would use in a rage room or for stupid science experiments. Either way I wouldn't recommend this microwave to anyone unless they are on a budget.
